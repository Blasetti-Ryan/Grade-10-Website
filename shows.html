#2:Family Feud

My second favourite game show is Family Feud. This show has two teams of five contestants that make up a team/family. These two teams go back and forth to attempt to guess the answers people have given to a survey. If they get it right, they get points and the first to reach 300 poits gets to play the bonus round. In this bonus round (called the fast-money round) contestants have 20 seconds to guess the highest answers of a survey. If they reach over 200 points, they win $20,000.


This show makes it into my Best of because of the enjoyment I get from playing along. When watching this show, you are not given the answers until the end. This makes it fun to watch with family or friends so you can try to guess the answers before the contestants do. It is a really satisfying felling to get it right, especially the hard answers. Furthermore, the host Steve Harvey is very funny and entertaining, keeping the viewer's attention.

#1:Big Brother

My favourite game show is Big Brother. This is a game show about a bunch of people who are brought into a house and sequestered from society. Their objective is to be the last one in the house. Every week, houseguests compete for the head of household. This power allows them to put two people on the block. These two people plus four more compete in a veto competition, and the prize can be used to take someone off of the block. At the end of the week, the other houseguests vote for which of the people on the block they want to go home. Whoever recieves the most votes is evicted.


This show makes it into my Best of because of the excitement. It's always interesting to watch this show because the competition's are so fun and entertaining to watch. Additionally, most people will pick someone to root for and it's always awesome to watch them win. The best part is watching people make alliances and then waiting to see who turns on the others first.

#2:The Middle

My second favourite comedy show is The Middle. This show is a sitcom about a family. The family always gets into arguments or comedic scenarios with one another that resolves itself at the end of the show, leaving a funny, but heart warming message. The family is made up of the two parents (Frankie and Mike Heck) and three children (Sue, Axl,and Brick Heck).


This show makes it into my Best of because of the comedy and relatability. This show has some witty dialogue and makes everything work together smoothly which allows for easy joke setup. Additionally, it's funnier because the show is so relatable to the life of my family. When watching it with family it's fun to see which character is most like each family member. Finally, the best part is the heartwarming message at the end that wrpas up the episode.

#1:Impractical Jokers

My favourite comedy show is Impractical Jokers. This show is about four guys (named Q, Murr, Sal, and Joe) who set tasks that they all compete in. These tasks usually include doing difficult or embarassing things to the public that do not know about it. The person that fails gets a loss and at the end of the show, the person with the most losses faces an ultimate punsihment.


This show makes it into my Best of because of the tasks. In this show, the tasks that they must complete usually create funny situations since the public does not know it is only for a televsion show. In some scenarios, they get scared they may get hurt by someone. In conclusion, the ultimate punsihments are the funniest things to watch.
